This repository contains a collection of independently developed investment research reports, financial analyses, and valuation projects focused on publicly traded companies, private firms, and emerging market opportunities.
The objective of these projects is to demonstrate practical application of financial analysis, investment research, valuation methodologies, economic reasoning, and risk assessment. Reports are developed using publicly available financial statements & 10Ks, SEC filings, earnings presentations, investor communications, industry research, and macroeconomic data.
